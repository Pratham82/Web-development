1. Define you project:
    Define the goal of you project
    Define your audience
    Design with you goal and audience in mind

2. Plan out your content
    Plan your context: txt, images, videos and icons
    Start thinking about visual hierarchy
    Define the navigation
    Define the site structure if its a big project

3. Sketch your ideas before you Design
    Get inspired and think about your Design
    Get th ideas out of your head: sketch your ideas before designing
    Make as many sketches as you want but don't make it perfect 
    Never start designing without having an idea og what you want to build

4. Design and deploy your website 
    Design your website using guidelines and tips you have learned in web design sections.
    Do that using HTML and CSS designing in the browser
    Use sketches, content and planning decisions you've made in steps 1,2 and 3
    This step is most important

5. Optimization
    Optimization performance: site speed 
    Search engine Optimization(SEO)

6. All you need for launching is web server that will host your website and deliver it to the world

7. Site maintenance
    Launching is not the end of the story
    Monitor your users behavior and change your website if necessary
    Update your content regularly: blog
